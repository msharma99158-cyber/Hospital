Project Overview

This project is designed to help hospitals manage patient information during emergency situations when family members are not available. It provides a digital platform to collect, store, and access patient details securely and efficiently.

In addition to patient information management, the system also supports hospital bed availability checking and ambualance booking. This feature is especially useful in rural areas where finding available hospital beds and ambulances during emergencies can be difficult. The system helps reduse delays and improve access to timely medical care.

Objectives

  • Reduce paperwork during emergencies
  • Save time for hospital staff
  • Ensure accurate patient data storage
  • Improve emergency healthcare services

Key Features

  • Online patient information form
  • Emergency contact details
  • Hospital bed booking system
  • Ambulance availability and booking
  • User-friendly interface
